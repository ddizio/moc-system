Start a New MOC

Select the type of change you want to manage. Use the MOC Hazard Assessment to determine the correct level.

L1
Level 1 MOC
Low risk, low hazard change. Single-page approval with Process Safety + Relevant Manager.
L2
Level 2 MOC (also for L3 / L4)
High significance or high hazard. Full approval chain — PM, TA, Maintenance, EHSS, Process Safety. PHA may be required.
⛚
Management of Organizational Change
Personnel change — resignation, transfer, or new assignment. Requires a transition plan and Site Manager approval.
!
Safety System Bypass / Impairment
Temporary bypass of a safety device. Requires an alternate protection plan and daily verification log.
+
MOC Addendum
Add a scope change to an existing approved MOC. Requires Technical Authority sign-off.
